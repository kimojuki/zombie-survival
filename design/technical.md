# TECHNICAL_RULES.md

# Technical Generation Rules

## Rendering Philosophy

Optimize for:

* large open world
* many zombies
* stable framerate

Use:

* low-poly assets
* aggressive LODs
* simple materials
* lightweight foliage

---

# Environment Density Rules

Avoid large empty flat terrain.

Every terrain chunk should contain:

* trees
* rocks
* debris
* structures
* roads
* landmarks
* elevation variation

---

# Terrain Rules

Use:

* hills
* rivers
* coastlines
* forests
* open fields

Avoid:

* perfectly flat terrain
* repetitive placement

---

# Atmospheric Rules

The world should feel:

* lonely
* post-apocalyptic
* abandoned
* tense

Use:

* fog
* distant silhouettes
* broken infrastructure
* empty roads
* isolated sounds

---

# Gameplay Distribution

Safe areas:

* forests
* farms
* cabins

Medium danger:

* villages
* gas stations
* roads

High danger:

* city
* industrial zone
* military base

---

# Exploration Philosophy

The player should always wonder:
"What is that structure in the distance?"

Landmarks must be visible from far away:

* water tower
* radio tower
* lighthouse
* factory chimney
* skyscrapers
* mountain silhouette
