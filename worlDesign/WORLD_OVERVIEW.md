# Zombie Survival World Overview

## Goal

Create a large open-world zombie survival map with a low-poly stylized visual style.

The world must feel:

* abandoned
* believable
* explorable
* dangerous
* atmospheric

The map should NEVER feel empty.

The player should constantly see:

* roads
* electrical poles
* small structures
* abandoned vehicles
* isolated houses
* farms
* forests
* industrial areas
* road signs
* ruins
* landmarks in the distance

---

# Visual Style

Keep the exact same visual direction as the current prototype:

* low-poly models
* simple geometry
* soft colors
* stylized trees
* minimal textures
* clean silhouettes
* slightly foggy atmosphere
* large visibility distance

Avoid:

* realistic textures
* overly detailed assets
* photorealism

The world should resemble:

* Unturned
* low-poly DayZ
* stylized Project Zomboid in 3D

---

# World Composition

The map must contain:

## Main City

Large urban area:

* apartment buildings
* supermarkets
* parking lots
* police station
* gas station
* hospital
* destroyed streets

This zone has:

* high zombie density
* high loot quality

---

## Small Villages

Scattered around the map.

Each village should contain:

* 5 to 15 houses
* a few shops
* abandoned cars
* utility poles
* fences
* gardens

---

## Industrial Zone

Contains:

* warehouse buildings
* factory
* containers
* trucks
* storage areas

Dangerous but high-value loot.

---

## Military Base

Located far from spawn areas.

Contains:

* barracks
* fences
* watch towers
* checkpoints
* military tents

Highest loot quality.
Highest zombie danger.

---

## Rural Areas

Large fields and forests between towns.

Should include:

* farms
* barns
* silos
* dirt roads
* isolated cabins

These areas create exploration and survival atmosphere.

---

# Important Rule

The player should NEVER walk for more than 20–30 seconds without discovering:

* a structure
* a road
* a landmark
* or environmental storytelling.
